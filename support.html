Support

User Forum

The best source of help in setting up your station or configuring WSJT-X, MAP65, or QMAP is the WSJTX GROUP at URL https://wsjtx.groups.io/g/main/messages. It is an active forum for communication among users of all the WSJT group of programs. Someone with similar interests and equipment may have already solved your problem and will be happy to help. To post messages you will need to join the group.

Bug Reports

Bugs can  be reported to the WSJT Developers email list (wsjt-devel@lists.sourceforge.net). Here too, you will need to subscribe to the list in order to post messages. To be useful, bug reports should include at least the following information:

  • Program version
  • Operating system
  • Concise description of the problem
  • Exact sequence of steps required to reproduce the problem
